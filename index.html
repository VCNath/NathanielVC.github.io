About Me

Welcome to my data analytics world! I'm Nathaniel, an economics major turned data analyst with a strong focus on finance and business analysis.

As a novice data analyst, I am passionate about uncovering meaningful insights from complex datasets to drive strategic decision-making. I bring a dependable and dedicated approach to my work, always striving to deliver accurate and actionable results.

With a solid background in economics, I understand the intricacies of financial markets and the importance of data-driven insights in driving business success. I leverage my analytical skills and expertise in statistical modeling to extract valuable information that helps organizations identify opportunities, mitigate risks, and optimize their financial performance.

I am proficient in using tools such as Python and R for data analysis and visualization. I continuously expand my knowledge in the field of data analytics, staying up to date with the latest industry trends and techniques.

My goal is to assist businesses in making informed decisions based on data, enabling them to achieve their financial objectives and thrive in a competitive landscape.
